Information regarding EUS and Covid-19

Please keep checking this page for the most up-to-date information regarding EUS and its services

Academics

  • Course Delivery: As it stands, the Faculty is currently planning for courses to be completely remote for the Fall semester and most students are not permitted to enter buildings on campus.
  • Tuition: Fees will not be lowered nor increased for the Fall semester, regardless of the course delivery.
  • Leave of absence: The University has not modified its policies to make it easier for students to take leaves of absences, which means most students have to return for the Fall semester.
  • Tech Fair: The career fair will be taking place in the Fall under a modified format.
  • EPTS: The peer tutoring service will be active during the Fall under a modified format.

For more information contact VP Academic

Events

  • We are determined to uphold the sense of community and support within our community here at McGill Engineering. We are following the government regulations closely to ensure the safety of everyone.
  • We are hoping to be able to continue the operations of our event-based committees (Frosh, E-Week, MERTW/Winter, Blues Pub, EAC, ESC, EUS Sports and Ski Trip) as long as the government regulations permit us. In the meantime make sure to follow your favourite committees on socials for some virtual fun and more updates!
    • Due to the strictly limited access to campus we will not be holding any in-person Blues Pubs over the summer until further notice.
    • Make sure to follow the Engineering Frosh Facebook Page to see the statement regarding Frosh 2020: Engineering Frosh Facebook Page
  • If you are an incoming student for the upcoming 2020-2021 academic year, make sure to join the Official Engineering Incoming Class of 2020 Facebook Group to connect with us and the Class of 2020: Engineering Incoming Class of 2020 Facebook Group

For more information contact VP Internal
